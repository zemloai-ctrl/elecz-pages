What is Elecz?

Elecz turns real-time electricity prices into actionable signals — for AI agents, home automation, and anyone whose decisions depend on when they use electricity.

Markets: Finland · Sweden · Norway · Denmark · Germany · Netherlands · Belgium · Austria · France · Italy · Poland · Czech Republic · Hungary · Romania · Spain · Portugal · Croatia · Bulgaria · Slovenia · Slovakia · Greece · Estonia · Latvia · Lithuania · Switzerland · Serbia · Bosnia · Montenegro · North Macedonia · United Kingdom · Australia · New Zealand

Elecz provides price signals. Scheduling decisions — deadlines, device constraints, priorities — remain with the caller.

MCP Tools

ToolWhen to useReturns
spot_price User asks what electricity costs right now Current price in local unit (NZD c/kWh, AUD c/kWh, p/kWh, c/kWh, ore/kWh)
cheapest_hours User asks when to run appliances, charge EV, schedule tasks Cheapest slots + best consecutive window next 24h (not available for AU or NZ)
best_energy_contract User asks which contract to choose or whether to switch provider Best dynamic/spot contract, best fixed contract, and recommended option

REST API

Base URL: https://elecz.com — No authentication. No rate limit for reasonable use.

EndpointDescription
GET /signal/spot?zone=FICurrent spot price only
GET /signal/cheapest-hours?zone=FI&hours=5Cheapest hours next 24h
GET /signal?zone=FI&consumption=2000Full signal with contract recommendations
GET /go/<provider>Redirect to provider + analytics
GET /healthHealth check
GET /signal/optimize?zone=FIDeprecated — use /signal

🇦🇺 Australia

AU spot prices sourced from AEMO — 5-minute NEM dispatch prices, updated every 30 min. Unit: AUD c/kWh.

Zones: AU-NSW · AU-VIC · AU-QLD · AU-SA · AU-TAS

Note: cheapest_hours returns available: false — no public day-ahead data for AU.

curl "https://elecz.com/signal/spot?zone=AU-NSW"
curl "https://elecz.com/signal?zone=AU-VIC&consumption=4500"

🇳🇿 New Zealand

NZ spot prices sourced from EM6 real-time API — NZEM wholesale prices, 30-minute trading periods, updated every 30 min. Unit: NZD c/kWh.

Zones: NZ-NI (North Island) · NZ-SI (South Island)

Providers: Mercury Energy · Contact Energy · Genesis Energy · Meridian Energy · Electric Kiwi

Note: No spot pass-through retailers currently available. Flick Electric was acquired by Meridian Energy (July 2025).

curl "https://elecz.com/signal/spot?zone=NZ-NI"
curl "https://elecz.com/signal?zone=NZ-NI&consumption=8000"

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

GB spot prices sourced from Octopus Agile API — half-hourly resolution, updated every 30 min. Unit: p/kWh incl. VAT (5%).

Default zone GB = London (region C). Regional zones: GB-A through GB-P.

curl "https://elecz.com/signal/spot?zone=GB"
curl "https://elecz.com/signal?zone=GB&consumption=2700"

🇩🇪 Germany

DE spot prices from ENTSO-E. Contract comparison includes 12 providers. Unit: EUR c/kWh.

Note: Price excludes regional Netzentgelt (varies ~10–15 ct/kWh by area).

curl "https://elecz.com/signal?zone=DE&consumption=3500"

Data Sources

Nordic + Central + Southern Europe spot from ENTSO-E, updated hourly. GB from Octopus Agile API, every 30 min. AU from AEMO, every 30 min. NZ from EM6, every 30 min. Contract prices scraped nightly via Gemini. Currency conversion via Frankfurter API.
